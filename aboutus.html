About Us

At Clean Gomti NGO, we are dedicated to the restoration and preservation of the iconic Gomti River that flows through the heart of Lucknow. Our mission is to revive the Gomti's pristine beauty, making it a source of life, recreation, and pride for the residents of Lucknow once again. With unwavering commitment and collaborative efforts, we are striving to address the challenges that have plagued this historic river and bring about positive change.

Our Aim

Our primary goal is to rejuvenate the Gomti River, focusing on the following key areas:

  • Water Quality Improvement: Through consistent monitoring, awareness campaigns, and community participation, we are working towards enhancing the water quality of the Gomti River, ensuring a healthier ecosystem for both aquatic life and human use.
  • Waste Management:We aim to reduce pollution by implementing effective waste management strategies. We organize regular clean-up drives along the riverbanks and educate communities about responsible waste disposal practices.
  • Biodiversity Conservation: Protecting the diverse flora and fauna along the river is essential for a balanced ecosystem. Our initiatives involve planting native vegetation, creating green spaces, and promoting the habitat for local wildlife.
  • Community Engagement: We believe that a sustainable transformation can only be achieved through collective efforts. We engage with local communities, schools, and businesses to raise awareness, conduct workshops, and foster a sense of ownership towards the river's well-being.

About Us

GoForGomti is a dedicated non-governmental organization (NGO) committed to reviving and rejuvenating the Gomti River – a lifeline that sustains our city's heritage, culture, and environment. We have taken upon ourselves the responsibility of restoring the Gomti River to its former glory by addressing the challenges it faces and fostering a community-driven movement towards its preservation.
