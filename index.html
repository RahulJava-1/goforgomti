Join Us As a Champion

Are you ready to be a part of something bigger than yourself? Introducing the Go for Gomti Campaign – a movement that goes beyond being mere volunteers; we are all Champions in the fight for a cleaner, healthier Gomti River.
Why a Champion? We believe that every individual who joins hands with us isn't just contributing time and effort, but embodying the spirit of change. When you participate in the Go for Gomti Campaign, you become a champion of the river's restoration. You don't just volunteer – you lead by example, inspire others, and make a lasting impact. That's why we proudly call all participants 'Champions.'

Our Mission

Our mission is to rejuvenate and restore the pristine beauty of the Gomti River in Lucknow, creating a sustainable and thriving ecosystem for the benefit of present and future generations. We are committed to cleaning, revitalizing, and maintaining the Gomti River's water quality, ensuring it becomes a symbol of environmental health and a source of pride for our community. Through active engagement, collaboration, and innovative solutions, we aim to foster awareness and action to safeguard the river's ecosystem, making it a beacon of environmental conservation and a source of inspiration for the entire region.

Our Vision

Our vision is of a Gomti River that flows with crystal-clear waters, teeming with diverse aquatic life, surrounded by lush greenery, and cherished by the community as a precious natural heritage. We envision a future where the Gomti River is a vibrant hub for recreational activities, a sanctuary for wildlife, and a source of clean water for the city of Lucknow. Through our relentless efforts, we aspire to transform the Gomti River into a model of sustainable water management, demonstrating the power of collective action and responsible environmental stewardship. Our success will not only benefit the river ecosystem but will also serve as a reminder that the commitment to nature's well-being can bring about positive change that resonates far beyond its banks.

How did it all start

Empowering Change: The Go for Gomti Initiative

In the heart of Lucknow, a transformative journey began with a simple thought – to cleanse and rejuvenate the lifeline of our city, the Gomti River. This vision was the brainchild of none other than RJ Prateek Sir, the dynamic President of Go for Gomti.
The inspiration struck when RJ Prateek Sir tuned into the nation's pulse through the Prime Minister's Mann Ki Baat. In that episode, the tale of Jadav Payeng, fondly known as the "Forest Man," unfolded. His remarkable feat of transforming Assam's Majuli Island with a sprawling forest sparked a fire within RJ Prateek Sir. He envisioned a similar transformation for Gomti River and its banks.
With a vision and determination that resonated with the city's top social media influencers, RJ Prateek Sir and his team embarked on the Go for Gomti campaign. What began as an idea quickly evolved into a movement. Over a hundred passionate individuals soon joined the cause, turning the initiative into a force to be reckoned with.
The efforts of Go for Gomti did not go unnoticed. The Defence Minister of India, Shri Rajnath Singh Ji, and the Mayor of Lucknow, Shrimati Sushma Kharakwal, extended their support and appreciation for the initiative. The Lucknow Municipal Corporation recognized the impact of RJ Prateek Sir's leadership and designated him as the Brand Ambassador of LMC.
The journey of Go for Gomti has been marked by remarkable achievements. With unwavering dedication, the initiative has successfully removed tons of plastic waste and hyacinth from the river, restoring its natural beauty. Through 15+ clean-up drives, the Go for Gomti team has shown that a united community can bring about tangible change.
The green revolution along the Gomti's banks has also been a priority. Over 100+ trees have been planted, creating a thriving ecosystem that will endure for generations. The support from Lete Hue Hanuman Mandir's Dr. Vivek Tangri Ji and his management team has been invaluable in this endeavor.
In this transformative journey, partnerships have played a crucial role. Dr. Momos has emerged as the initiative's fooding partner, nourishing the champions on the frontlines. Ark Savi, the digital partner, has been instrumental in spreading the message far and wide, igniting a digital movement to complement the on-ground efforts.
Go for Gomti isn't just an initiative; it's a movement for change. RJ Prateek Sir's unwavering commitment and the collective efforts of countless individuals have transformed a dream into a reality. The Gomti River is no longer just a waterway – it's a symbol of resilience, community, and hope.
Join us in our pursuit of a cleaner, greener, and more vibrant Gomti River. Be a part of Go for Gomti – where every individual is a champion of change.

About Us

GoForGomti is a dedicated non-governmental organization (NGO) committed to reviving and rejuvenating the Gomti River – a lifeline that sustains our city's heritage, culture, and environment. We have taken upon ourselves the responsibility of restoring the Gomti River to its former glory by addressing the challenges it faces and fostering a community-driven movement towards its preservation.
