About Us

GoForGomti is a dedicated non-governmental organization (NGO) committed to reviving and rejuvenating the Gomti River – a lifeline that sustains our city's heritage, culture, and environment. We have taken upon ourselves the responsibility of restoring the Gomti River to its former glory by addressing the challenges it faces and fostering a community-driven movement towards its preservation.
